About Fubo TV

Fubo is a live television streaming service that offers sports channels, entertainment programs, movies, and news coverage. After subscribing, users can manage everything from their personal account dashboard.

Your Fubo TV login account allows you to:

- Manage subscriptions

How to Sign In to Your Account
==============================

Accessing your account is simple when you follow the correct steps.

Open the official Fubo website or launch the mobile application.  
Select the sign in option.  
Enter your registered email address carefully.  
Type your password correctly.  
Proceed to access your account dashboard.

Always confirm that you are using the official platform before entering login credentials.

Signing In on Different Devices
===============================

Fubo supports multiple devices. The sign-in experience may vary slightly depending on your device.

Smart Television
----------------

Open the Fubo application on your television.  
Choose sign in.  
Enter your credentials using your remote or follow activation instructions if displayed.

Mobile Device
-------------

Download and install the Fubo app from your device store.  
Open the app and tap sign in.  
Enter your email and password.

Web Browser
-----------

Visit the official Fubo website.  
Click sign in at the top section.  
Enter your login details and continue.

Using updated software helps reduce login problems.

Resetting Your Password
=======================

If you forget your password, recovery is straightforward.

Select forgot password on the login page.  
Enter your registered email address.  
Check your inbox for password reset instructions.  
Create a new strong password.  
Confirm changes and sign in again.

Choose a secure password that combines letters, symbols, and unique phrasing.

Common Login Problems and Solutions
====================================

Incorrect Credentials
---------------------

Ensure your email spelling is correct. Disable caps lock and re-enter your password carefully.

Browser Issues
--------------

Clear browser cache and cookies. Restart the browser and try again.

Application Errors
------------------

Update the Fubo app to the latest available version to prevent compatibility problems.

Temporary Account Lock
----------------------

Multiple failed attempts may temporarily restrict access. Wait and retry later or reset your password.

Subscription Concerns
---------------------

If your subscription has expired, renew it from your account dashboard to restore streaming access.

Keeping Your Account Secure
===========================

Account safety protects your personal and billing information.

- Use a strong and unique password  
- Avoid sharing login credentials  
- Sign out from public devices  
- Monitor account activity  
- Remove unknown devices  

If suspicious activity appears, update your password immediately.

Managing Devices
================

You can manage active devices directly inside your account settings.

Sign in to your account.  
Open account settings.  
Navigate to the device management section.  
Remove any unfamiliar or unused devices.

This ensures better control over streaming access.
